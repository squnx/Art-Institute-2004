The Art Institute of California-Los Angeles opened its location on 31st Street in the city of Santa Monica in 1997 with a mission to provide programs that will prepare students for careers in art-, design-, and culinary-related fields.

The Art Institute of California-Los Angeles offers year-round programs that benefit from the support and contributions from the professional community. An impressive faculty of working professionals strives to strengthen students' skills and to cultivate their talents. Curricula are reviewed periodically to ensure they meet the needs of a changing marketplace—preparing students for entry-level positions in creative, challenging careers.
